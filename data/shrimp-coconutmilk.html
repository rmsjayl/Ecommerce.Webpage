Shrimp Coconut Milk

(4.5/5 based on 200 reviews)

Ingredients

  • 500g shrimp (head and shell intact or removed)
  • 2 cups coconut milk
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 pieces green chili (optional for spiciness)
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon cooking oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Kangkong (water spinach) or eggplant (optional)

Instructions

  1. Sauté the aromatics: In a pan, heat oil over medium heat. Add minced garlic and chopped onion, sautéing until fragrant and the onion becomes translucent, creating a flavorful base for the dish.
  2. Cook the shrimp: Add the shrimp to the pan and cook for 2-3 minutes, or until they turn pink and opaque. Once cooked, set the shrimp aside to prevent overcooking.
  3. Simmer with coconut milk: Pour in the coconut milk and add fish sauce, pepper, and green chili. Bring the mixture to a simmer and cook for 5-7 minutes, allowing the sauce to thicken slightly while the flavors meld together.
  4. Return the shrimp: Add the cooked shrimp back into the pan and simmer for an additional 2 minutes, allowing them to absorb the rich flavors of the coconut milk sauce.
  5. Add optional vegetables: If desired, stir in kangkong (water spinach) or sliced eggplant for added texture and nutrition. Cook until the vegetables are tender.
  6. Season and serve: Taste the dish and adjust the seasoning with salt as needed. Serve hot alongside rice for a delicious and satisfying meal.
