Pancit

(4.5/5 based on 200 reviews)

Ingredients

  • 250g pancit bihon (rice noodles)
  • 1/4 kg chicken or pork, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1/2 cabbage, shredded
  • 2 carrots, julienned
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp fish sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 cups chicken broth

Instructions

  1. Soak the noodles: Soak noodles for 10 minutes, then drain them well.
  2. Sauté the aromatics: Sauté garlic and onion in a pan until fragrant. Add chicken or pork and cook until browned.
  3. Add the vegetables: Add your choice of vegetables and cook until tender.
  4. Pour in the liquids: Pour broth, soy sauce, and fish sauce into the pan. Allow to simmer to combine the flavors.
  5. Add the noodles: Finally, add the soaked noodles and cook until they are soft and fully incorporated.
