Chicken Adobo

Ingredients

  • 1 kg beef (cut into cubes)
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1/4 cup liver spread
  • 1 carrot (sliced)
  • 1 potato (cubed)
  • 1 bell pepper (sliced)
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 4 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 cup water
  • 2-3 pieces bay leaves
  • Olives and grated cheese (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cooking oil

Instructions

  1. Marinate the meat: Combine pork or chicken with soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, peppercorns, and bay leaves in a bowl. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to penetrate the meat, ensuring a rich and savory taste.
  2. Sauté the onions: Heat oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add the onion and sauté until it becomes soft and translucent, which will provide a fragrant base for the dish.
  3. Brown the marinated meat: Add the marinated meat to the pan, reserving the marinade for later use. Cook until the meat is browned on all sides, creating a flavorful crust that enhances the overall taste.
  4. Simmer the dish: Pour in the reserved marinade and add water to the pan. Stir to combine and bring to a gentle simmer. Cook for 30-45 minutes until the meat is tender and infused with the aromatic flavors.
  5. Adjust the seasoning: Taste the dish and adjust the seasoning with salt, pepper, or additional soy sauce and vinegar as needed. This step ensures the flavors are balanced to your liking.
