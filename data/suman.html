Suman

Ingredients

  • 2 cups glutinous rice
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • Banana leaves (for wrapping)

Instructions

  1. Soak the rice: Soak rice for 4 hours, then drain well.
  2. Cook the rice: In a pot, cook coconut milk and sugar together. Add the drained rice and cook until fully absorbed.
  3. Wrap and steam: Wrap the cooked rice mixture in banana leaves and steam for 45 minutes until set.
  4. Serve: Once cooked, serve with sugar or grated coconut for added sweetness.
