Maja Jubilee

Ingredients

  • 1 can corn kernels
  • 1/2 cup cornstarch
  • 2 cups coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup condensed milk

Instructions

  1. Combine the ingredients: In a pot, mix coconut milk, sugar, and cornstarch. Stir until smooth and well combined.
  2. Cook the mixture: Add corn and cook over medium heat, stirring constantly until thickened. This will create a rich and creamy texture.
  3. Cool and serve: Pour the mixture into a greased dish and let it cool. Drizzle with condensed milk before serving for added sweetness.
