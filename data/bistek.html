Bistek

(4.5/5 based on 200 reviews)

Ingredients

  • 500g beef sirloin or round steak (thinly sliced)
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 4-5 pieces calamansi (or 2 tablespoons lemon juice)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 large onion (sliced into rings)
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • Salt and additional pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Marinate the beef: In a bowl, combine the soy sauce, calamansi (or lemon juice), minced garlic, and pepper. Add the beef slices and marinate for at least 30 minutes, or up to 2 hours for more flavor.
  2. Cook the beef: Heat 1 tablespoon of oil in a pan over medium heat. Remove the beef from the marinade (reserve the marinade) and fry the slices in batches, cooking each side for about 2-3 minutes or until browned. Set aside the cooked beef.
  3. Sauté the onions: In the same pan, add another tablespoon of oil. Sauté the onion rings until they are soft and translucent. Remove from the pan and set aside.
  4. Simmer in the marinade: Pour the reserved marinade into the pan and add the water. Bring it to a simmer, then return the beef slices to the pan. Cook for another 10-15 minutes or until the beef is tender and the sauce slightly thickens.
  5. Combine and serve: Top the beef with sautéed onions, then serve the Bistek hot with steamed rice.
