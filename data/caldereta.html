Caldereta

Ingredients

  • 1 kg beef (cut into cubes)
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1/4 cup liver spread
  • 1 carrot (sliced)
  • 1 potato (cubed)
  • 1 bell pepper (sliced)
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 4 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 cup water
  • 2-3 pieces bay leaves
  • Olives and grated cheese (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cooking oil

Instructions

  1. Sauté the aromatics: Heat oil in a pan and add garlic and onion. Sauté until softened and fragrant, releasing their natural sweetness to enhance the dish's flavor.
  2. Brown the beef: Add the beef to the pan, ensuring it is spread out in a single layer. Cook until it is browned on all sides, sealing in the juices for a more flavorful dish.
  3. Simmer the sauce: Pour in the tomato sauce, water, and soy sauce. Add bay leaves and stir to combine. Bring to a gentle simmer and let it cook for 1-2 hours until the beef is tender and the flavors meld together beautifully.
  4. Incorporate liver spread: Add liver spread to the pot and stir well, allowing it to dissolve and enrich the sauce with its creamy texture and savory depth.
  5. Add the vegetables: Toss in the potatoes, carrots, and bell peppers. Cook until the vegetables are soft and tender, absorbing the delicious flavors of the stew.
  6. Finish with olives and cheese: Stir in olives and, if desired, grated cheese for an extra layer of richness. Season with salt and pepper to taste, adjusting the flavors to your preference.
  7. Serve and enjoy: Serve the dish hot over a bed of rice, allowing the savory sauce to soak into the grains for a comforting and satisfying meal.
