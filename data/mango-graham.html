Mango Graham

Ingredients

  • 4 ripe mangoes, sliced
  • 1 pack Graham crackers
  • 1 can all-purpose cream
  • 1/2 cup condensed milk

Instructions

  1. Mix the ingredients: Combine cream and condensed milk in a bowl until well blended.
  2. Layer the ingredients: In a dish, layer Graham crackers, the cream mixture, and mango slices. Make sure to spread the cream evenly.
  3. Repeat and chill: Repeat the layers, finishing with a final layer of mango slices on top. Chill for 4 hours or overnight to allow the flavors to meld.
