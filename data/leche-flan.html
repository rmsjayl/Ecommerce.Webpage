Leche flan

(4.5/5 based on 200 reviews)

Ingredients

  • 10 egg yolks
  • 1 can condensed milk
  • 1 can evaporated milk
  • 1 cup sugar (for caramel)
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Caramelize the sugar: Begin by heating sugar in a pan over medium heat, stirring gently until it melts and turns a rich amber color. Carefully pour the melted sugar into llaneras (molds), swirling to coat the bottom evenly. Allow the caramel to harden as it cools, forming a sweet layer that will add flavor to your dessert.
  2. Prepare the custard mixture: In a mixing bowl, whisk together the egg yolks, condensed milk, evaporated milk, and a splash of vanilla extract until well combined. Ensure there are no lumps and the mixture is smooth, as this will create a creamy custard.
  3. Steam the mixture: Pour the custard mixture into the prepared molds over the hardened caramel. Set up a steamer or place the molds in a pot with a little water, ensuring the water doesn't touch the mixture. Steam for 30-40 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  4. Cool and invert: Once steamed, carefully remove the molds from the heat and let them cool to room temperature. Once cooled, run a knife around the edges to loosen the custard, then invert onto a plate. The caramel layer should flow beautifully over the custard, creating a delicious dessert that’s ready to serve!
