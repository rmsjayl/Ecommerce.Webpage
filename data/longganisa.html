Longganisa-Hamonado

Ingredients

  • 500g ground pork
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 5 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground pepper
  • 1 tablespoon vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon salt

Instructions

  1. Prepare the sausage mixture: In a large bowl, combine the ground pork, sugar, soy sauce, minced garlic, pepper, vinegar, and salt. Mix well until all ingredients are thoroughly incorporated, ensuring a balanced flavor throughout the sausage.
  2. Stuff the sausages: Carefully stuff the mixture into sausage casings, ensuring there are no air pockets, or shape the mixture by hand into patties or links if preferred. Make sure to seal the ends of the casings tightly to keep the filling intact.
  3. Cook the sausages: Heat a pan or grill over medium heat and add a little oil if needed. Cook the sausages until they are caramelized on the outside and fully cooked through, turning occasionally for even browning and flavor development.
  4. Serve and enjoy: Serve the delicious sausages hot with a side of fried rice and eggs, creating a satisfying and hearty meal that combines a variety of flavors and textures.
