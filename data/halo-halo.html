Halo halo

(4.5/5 based on 200 reviews)

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup each of various sweetened ingredients (bananas, beans, nata de coco, kaong, macapuno, etc.)
  • Shaved ice
  • 1/4 cup evaporated milk
  • 1 scoop ube or vanilla ice cream (optional)
  • Leche flan (optional)
  • Pinipig (crispy rice, optional)

Instructions

  1. Layer the sweetened ingredients: Start by carefully layering your sweetened ingredients in a tall glass. You can use a combination of fruits, sweetened beans, or jellies, creating colorful layers for an attractive presentation.
  2. Top with shaved ice: Generously top the layered ingredients with a mound of finely shaved ice, allowing it to sit on top and create a fluffy texture. Next, pour evaporated milk over the shaved ice, letting it soak through to enhance the sweetness and creaminess of the dessert.
  3. Add the finishing touches: For an extra indulgent treat, add a scoop of your favorite ice cream on top, followed by slices of leche flan and a sprinkle of pinipig (crispy rice flakes) if desired. This adds a delightful crunch and richness to the dessert. Serve immediately to enjoy the contrasting textures and flavors.
