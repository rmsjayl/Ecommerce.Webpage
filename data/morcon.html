Morcon

(4.5/5 based on 200 reviews)

Ingredients

  • 1 kg beef flank or sirloin
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 hard-boiled eggs (sliced)
  • 1 hotdog or chorizo (cut lengthwise)
  • Pickles, cheese, and carrots (cut into strips)
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 4 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1/4 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 tablespoon cooking oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Marinate the beef: In a bowl, combine the beef with soy sauce, minced garlic, and pepper. Mix well to ensure the beef is evenly coated. Allow it to marinate for at least 1 hour, or longer for more intense flavor.
  2. Prepare the filling: Lay the marinated beef flat on a clean surface. Arrange the eggs, hotdog, pickles, cheese, and carrots in the center of the beef, creating a colorful and flavorful filling.
  3. Roll the beef: Carefully roll the beef tightly around the filling, ensuring everything is enclosed. Secure the roll with twine, tying it securely to maintain its shape during cooking.
  4. Brown the beef roll: In a hot pan, add a little oil and brown the beef roll on all sides until it develops a nice crust. This step adds flavor and locks in the juices. Once browned, set the roll aside.
  5. Prepare the sauce: In the same pan, saut√© minced garlic and onions until fragrant. Then, add tomato sauce and water, stirring to combine. Bring the mixture to a simmer, and carefully place the beef roll back into the pan. Cover and let it simmer for 1-2 hours, allowing the flavors to meld and the beef to become tender.
  6. Slice and serve: Once the beef roll is cooked and tender, remove it from the pan and slice it into rounds. Serve with the sauce spooned over the top for a delicious and hearty meal.
