Chicken Toccino

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken breast or thighs (thinly sliced)
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup pineapple juice
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon annatto powder (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil

Instructions

  1. Prepare the marinade: In a bowl, mix together the sugar, pineapple juice, salt, garlic powder, soy sauce, and annatto powder. Stir until everything is well combined and the sugar has fully dissolved. This vibrant marinade will give the chicken a balance of sweet, savory, and aromatic flavors.
  2. Marinate the chicken: Add the chicken pieces to the marinade, ensuring they are fully coated on all sides. Cover the bowl and let the chicken marinate in the refrigerator overnight, allowing the flavors to deeply penetrate the meat. The longer it marinates, the richer and more intense the flavor will be.
  3. Cook the chicken: Heat oil in a pan over medium heat until hot. Carefully place the marinated chicken in the pan, making sure not to overcrowd it. Cook until the chicken is caramelized and browned on all sides, turning occasionally. This will ensure the chicken develops a delicious, crispy exterior while remaining juicy inside.
  4. Serve and enjoy: Serve the beautifully caramelized chicken with a side of steamed rice and a perfectly fried egg. The combination of flavors and textures—the tender chicken, fluffy rice, and crispy egg—makes for a delicious and satisfying meal that is perfect for any occasion.
